about

To be Frank, I was and still am a writer, although just like you I do a bunch of other things everything from being a good dad, troubleshooting expeditions, doing up houses, travelling when possible and generally just trying to be present as much as possible.

On the writing front I’ve written everything from trad books to indie, political columns to feature articles, branded cities and hotel chains. I’ve been employed by household names like BBC, Natgeo, Guardian and Esquire to provide content as well as many others.

I’m a big fan of the outdoors, muck about with acting (it is storytelling with other people after all) and help people when I have the power.
